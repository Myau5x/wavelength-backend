Find and build your safe space
Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) is a mental health condition that affects 354 million people worldwide, yet is not as talked about as other mental health issues. Wavelength aims to offer a platform to victims of trauma for forming a community based support system.

How it works
Create an account
Click on the sign-up page, enter a username unique to you, picka password, write a short introduction about yourself and you're ready to go!
Find your pen pal
Finding your pen-pal is as easy as manuevring to the chat page and sending a connection request to someone who seems to match your wavelength.
Write a letter
Letters on Wavelength, inspired by traditional letters, take five to six hours to deliver to your pen-pal. Write a letter and await a response from your new friend the old-fashioned way!
